Climate–Agriculture Data Integration & Vulnerability Modeling

Built a multi-source country–year–crop dataset integrating climate and agricultural production data through automated SQL-based ETL workflows. Designed fixed-effects panel regression models with cluster-robust standard errors to quantify the impact of climate variables on crop yields and constructed a composite Food & Climate Vulnerability Index (FCVI) to rank country-level risk exposure.

SQL · R · Panel Regression · ETL · Data Visualization · Econometrics

Urban Heat & Green Infrastructure Analysis

Analyzed satellite data to quantify how green space reduces urban heat across Metro Vancouver. Built geospatial models showing that tree cover significantly lowers surface temperatures, but cooling effects weaken in high-density areas. Insights from this project support smarter urban planning decisions and targeted climate adaptation strategies.

Geospatial Analytics · Google Earth Engine · R · Data Modeling
